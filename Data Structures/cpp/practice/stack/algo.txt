brackets:
-for every character
-if its opening bracket , match its companion and push to stack
-if its closing bracket , pop from the stack
	- if popped does not equal the one incoming
		expression is invalid 
	- if it does 
		just pop

-check if stack is empty
	if not expression is also invalid


postfix conversion:
-make a priorit function
-func which checks open and closing bracket
-for every character
-if its alphabet or digit
	simply push to ret string
-if its opening bracket
	then push to stack
-if its closing bracket
	pop until stack is not empty and we find a opening bracket
-else
	-if its power symbol
	           simply push to stack
	-else
		pop and push to ret , while stack is not empty and priority of top is greater or equal
		to priority of current character
	after that just push the symbol to stack as it have find its right place
-in the end , pop all remaining elements from stack until its empty
